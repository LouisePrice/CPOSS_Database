The Database

Over the period 2003-2026, the CPOSS project carried out Crystal Structure Prediction on a larger number of small organic molecules. This included a smaller number of multi-component systems, such as cocrystals, salts, and hydrates. The crystal structures generated have been archived on Github, and are freely accessible to anyone.

Please refer to the instructions for how best to access this data.
