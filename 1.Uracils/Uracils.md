# 5-substituted uracils

(Last updated January 2026)

This is all the molecules covered in Sarah Barnett’s paper, [Barnett SA, Hulme AT, Issa N, Lewis TC, Price LS, Tocher DA, Price SL 2008. New J Chem 32, 1761-1775.:1761-1775.](https://pubs.rsc.org/en/content/articlelanding/2008/nj/b806763e)  5-fluorouracil is also covered in a separate file.  5-eniluracil’s search was not covered in that paper as it was covered in Copley RCB, Barnett SA, Karamertzanis PG, Harris KDM, Kariuki BM, Xu MC, Nickels EA, Lancaster RW, Price SL 2008.
