# Other notes

The structure matching is poor.  Structure matches with CrystalPredictor are outside RMSD<sub>30</sub>=0.3 Å (it was CrystalPredictor 1.9, so the molecule is made up of rigid fragments and is not a good match).  Matches to racemic form 2 are also not good, because the form 2 structure is poor.  Old manual matching is retained, rather than stating that nothing much matches and the searches were unsuccessful.
