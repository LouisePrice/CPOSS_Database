Lactide

(Last updated 24 March 2025)

Figure . The molecular diagram of Lactide.

This molecule has two stereocentres. meso-Lactide has one centre of each type, and hence the molecule is not chiral. Both chiral molecules (LL-Lactide and DD-Lactide) and the racemic mixture of the two have determinations on the CSD. Meso-Lactide was not included in the original CSP (it is a different molecule).
